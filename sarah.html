Sarah Johnson

Quick Facts

  • Award-winning Architect
  • Mother of Emma and Jack
  • Married to Michael for 15 years
  • Sustainability advocate
  • Amateur photographer

Sarah is the cornerstone of the Johnson family, balancing her successful career as an architect with being a devoted mother and wife. Her passion for sustainable design has led to several award-winning projects in the city, including the new Green Community Center.

Professional Life

As a senior partner at Johnson & Associates Architects, Sarah specializes in eco-friendly residential and community spaces. Her innovative approaches to sustainable architecture have earned her recognition in the field.

Family Life

Sarah's greatest joy comes from spending time with her family. She enjoys organizing weekend hiking trips, teaching Emma about photography, and helping Jack with his science projects.
